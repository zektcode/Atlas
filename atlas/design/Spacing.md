# Spacing

Base unit: 4px (Tailwind default scale). Investor-grade "expensive" feel comes from generosity at the macro level, not micro-spacing tricks:

- Section vertical padding: 80–120px (`py-20` to `py-28`) on marketing pages
- Card internal padding: 20–24px (`p-5` to `p-6`)
- Bento grid gap: 16px (`gap-4`) — tight enough to read as one system, loose enough that cards don't feel cramped
- Never let two cards touch with zero gap — the 1px border tokens rely on visible separation to read as elevation
