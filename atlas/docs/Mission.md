# Mission

**Help people make better financial decisions under uncertainty.**

Not by predicting the future. By quantifying uncertainty about it, honestly, and explaining the reasoning behind every number well enough that a user could argue with it.

## Operating principle

If a recommendation can't be decomposed into "here's what's known, here's what isn't, here's what would change my mind," it doesn't ship as a Decision Card — it ships as a caveat, or it doesn't ship.

## Who this mission serves first

Retail investors and swing traders who currently get either (a) no explanation, or (b) an explanation from a source with a conflict of interest (a broker upselling a product). Advisors and family offices are a real Phase 5 market, but the mission is proven or disproven with individual investors first — see `docs/PRD.md`.
